About Me

A quick overview of what I do and how I work.

Background

I work at the intersection of development and operations — building tooling and pipelines that help teams ship code safely and repeatedly. My day-to-day includes automating infrastructure, improving deployment confidence, and strengthening production stability with observability and incident response practices.

I’m especially focused on making the “easy path” the secure and reliable one — so developers can move fast without introducing risk.

Core Skills

  • CI/CD pipelines (GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, Jenkins-style workflows)
  • Infrastructure as Code (Terraform-style patterns, repeatable environments)
  • Containers & orchestration (Docker + Kubernetes fundamentals)
  • Cloud & networking basics (AWS-style services, IAM concepts, VPC patterns)
  • Observability (logs, metrics, traces, alerting and dashboards)
  • DevSecOps mindset (secrets hygiene, scanning, least privilege, secure defaults)

What I care about

Fewer surprises in production, faster delivery cycles, and clean systems that are easy to operate.
